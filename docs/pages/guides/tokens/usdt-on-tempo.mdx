# USDT on Tempo

USDT is the largest stablecoin by market capitalization, issued by Tether. On Tempo, you can send USDT with sub-cent fees, pay gas in stablecoins, and swap between currencies instantly.

## What is USDT?

USDT (Tether) is a stablecoin pegged 1:1 to the US dollar. Tether Limited issues USDT and maintains reserves in cash, cash equivalents, and other assets. Quarterly attestations report the composition of reserves backing USDT.

**Key facts:**
- **Issuer:** Tether Limited
- **Backing:** Cash, cash equivalents, US Treasury bills, and other assets
- **Peg:** 1 USDT = 1 USD
- **Market position:** Largest stablecoin by circulation

## Using USDT on Tempo

### Send USDT

Transfer USDT to any Tempo address with instant finality:

1. Connect your wallet to the Tempo app
2. Enter the recipient address and amount
3. Confirm the transaction (approximately 0.001 USD fee)
4. Funds arrive in approximately 0.5 seconds

### Receive USDT

Share your Tempo address to receive USDT. Incoming transfers finalize instantly and you can use funds immediately.

### Swap USDT

Convert USDT to other stablecoins on Tempo's native exchange:

- USDT → USDC
- USDT → EURC
- USDT → DAI
- USDT → PYUSD

Swaps execute at the oracle price with sub-cent fees.

## Why use USDT on Tempo?

### Stablecoin-native gas

Pay transaction fees in USDT—no ETH required. Your wallet deducts fees directly from your stablecoin balance.

### Sub-cent fees

Transaction fees on Tempo cost approximately 0.001 USD. Send 10 USD or 10,000 USD for the same fee.

### Instant finality

Transactions finalize in approximately 0.5 seconds. No waiting for block confirmations or pending states.

### Payment metadata

Attach invoice numbers, purchase orders, or references to any transfer for simplified reconciliation.

## Common use cases

- **Cross-border payments:** Send USDT internationally without banking delays
- **Merchant payments:** Accept USDT for goods and services
- **Remittances:** Transfer value globally with minimal fees
