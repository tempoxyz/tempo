# EURC on Tempo

EURC is Circle's euro-denominated stablecoin. On Tempo, you can send EURC with sub-cent fees, pay gas in stablecoins, and swap between currencies instantly.

## What is EURC?

EURC (Euro Coin) is a regulated stablecoin pegged 1:1 to the euro. Circle issues EURC and maintains reserves in euros held at regulated financial institutions. Monthly attestations by Deloitte verify the reserves backing every EURC in circulation.

**Key facts:**
- **Issuer:** Circle Internet Financial
- **Backing:** Euros held at regulated financial institutions
- **Peg:** 1 EURC = 1 EUR
- **Regulation:** Compliant with EU MiCA regulations

## Using EURC on Tempo

### Send EURC

Transfer EURC to any Tempo address with instant finality:

1. Connect your wallet to the Tempo app
2. Enter the recipient address and amount
3. Confirm the transaction (approximately 0.001 USD fee equivalent)
4. Funds arrive in approximately 0.5 seconds

### Receive EURC

Share your Tempo address to receive EURC. Incoming transfers finalize instantly and you can use funds immediately.

### Swap EURC

Convert EURC to other stablecoins on Tempo's native exchange:

- EURC → USDC
- EURC → USDT
- EURC → DAI
- EURC → PYUSD

Swaps execute at the oracle price with sub-cent fees. USD/EUR conversions use real-time exchange rates.

## Why use EURC on Tempo?

### Stablecoin-native gas

Pay transaction fees in EURC—no ETH required. Your wallet deducts fees directly from your stablecoin balance.

### Sub-cent fees

Transaction fees on Tempo cost approximately 0.001 USD equivalent. Send 10 EUR or 10,000 EUR for the same fee.

### Instant finality

Transactions finalize in approximately 0.5 seconds. No waiting for block confirmations or pending states.

### Payment metadata

Attach invoice numbers, purchase orders, or references to any transfer for simplified reconciliation.

## Common use cases

- **EUR-denominated payments:** Transact in euros without currency conversion
- **European commerce:** Accept payments from EU customers in their native currency
- **FX operations:** Swap between USD and EUR stablecoins at competitive rates
- **Cross-border EUR transfers:** Send euros internationally with instant settlement
- **Treasury diversification:** Hold both USD and EUR stablecoins
