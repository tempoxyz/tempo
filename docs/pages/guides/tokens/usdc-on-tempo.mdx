# USDC on Tempo

USDC is a fully-reserved dollar stablecoin issued by Circle. On Tempo, you can send USDC with sub-cent fees, pay gas in stablecoins, and swap between currencies instantly.

## What is USDC?

USDC (USD Coin) is a regulated stablecoin pegged 1:1 to the US dollar. Circle issues USDC and maintains reserves in cash and short-term US Treasury bonds. Monthly attestations by Deloitte verify the reserves backing every USDC in circulation.

**Key facts:**
- **Issuer:** Circle Internet Financial
- **Backing:** US dollars and short-term US Treasury bonds
- **Peg:** 1 USDC = 1 USD
- **Regulation:** Regulated as stored value under US money transmission laws

## Using USDC on Tempo

### Send USDC

Transfer USDC to any Tempo address with instant finality:

1. Connect your wallet to the Tempo app
2. Enter the recipient address and amount
3. Confirm the transaction (approximately 0.001 USD fee)
4. Funds arrive in approximately 0.5 seconds

### Receive USDC

Share your Tempo address to receive USDC. Incoming transfers finalize instantly and you can use funds immediately.

### Swap USDC

Convert USDC to other stablecoins on Tempo's native exchange:

- USDC → USDT
- USDC → EURC
- USDC → DAI
- USDC → PYUSD

Swaps execute at the oracle price with sub-cent fees.

## Why use USDC on Tempo?

### Stablecoin-native gas

Pay transaction fees in USDC—no ETH required. Your wallet deducts fees directly from your stablecoin balance.

### Sub-cent fees

Transaction fees on Tempo cost approximately 0.001 USD. Send 10 USD or 10,000 USD for the same fee.

### Instant finality

Transactions finalize in approximately 0.5 seconds. No waiting for block confirmations or pending states.

### Payment metadata

Attach invoice numbers, purchase orders, or references to any transfer for simplified reconciliation.
